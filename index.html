Who is Ryan Reiterman?

I was born in Columbus, Ohio. I attended Bowling Green State University and majored in journalism. After two years as a newspaper reporter, I landed a job with Golf.com as a digital producer. I was then promoted to a senior producer before jumping to Golf Channel as an assistant managing editor. After six years, I landed a contract role with Walt Disney World as a digital producer. Now I am focused on my next goal - becoming a front-end web developer.
